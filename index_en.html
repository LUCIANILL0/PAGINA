    About Us

    Our company is oriented towards the vision of being a sustainable company consolidated in the international market, producing organic food according to global standards and socially responsible in all its aspects. All our technique, passion and dedication are focused on providing healthy organic products with responsibility in their production, respecting the laws and the environment, and facilitating the development of its partners and collaborators.

    Our Certifications

    The certifications confirm to our clients, suppliers and collaborators that our company applies the best and recognized practices in the organic growth of different fruits.

    This certification guarantees that our products meet strict organic standards, ensuring that they have been grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, chemical fertilizers or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Thanks to this certification, we offer healthy and sustainable foods, aligned with the expectations of consumers looking for natural and environmentally friendly products.

    Organic

    This certification covers key aspects such as fair working conditions, respect for human rights, environmental sustainability and business ethics. Having SMETA reflects our commitment to responsible and transparent production, strengthening our reputation and trust in international markets.

    A nuisance

    This certification ensures that our products meet demanding criteria of sustainability, biodiversity and respect for the environment. It also guarantees that our farming and production practices promote a natural balance, avoiding the use of chemical pesticides and genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Bio Suisse allows us to access select markets and meet the demand of consumers who value the quality and responsible origin of organic products.

    Biosuisse

    This certification, especially relevant in the export of fresh fruit, guarantees compliance with the highest standards in quality, sustainability and good agricultural practices. It allows us to access a greater number of international markets, strengthen the trust of our customers and reaffirm our commitment to excellence at every stage of our production.

    Global Gap

    This certification ensures compliance with key standards in worker welfare, fair working conditions and respect for their rights. With GRASP, we reaffirm our commitment to social sustainability and corporate responsibility, strengthening the trust of our partners and customers in global markets.
